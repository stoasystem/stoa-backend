## Assets and trust boundaries

- Assets: plan identity, payment lifecycle truth, beneficiary scope, allowance evidence.
- Boundaries: browser/API JSON → Pydantic models; provider facts → internal models; internal projections → Web/admin JSON.

| Threat | STRIDE | Mitigation | Verification |
|---|---|---|---|
| Legacy alias broadens paid access | Tampering / Elevation | Closed enums and forbidden extras | Legacy-value parameterized tests |
| Redirect/provider hint becomes payment proof | Spoofing | Separate hint, fact, entitlement, and public-outcome models | Construction-negative tests |
| Sensitive payment or learning content enters evidence | Information disclosure | Closed evidence fields, no generic metadata payload | Canary-field rejection tests |
| Unbounded counts corrupt allowance | Tampering / DoS | Exact nonnegative integer validation | Bool/fraction/negative/oversize cases |
</threat_model>
