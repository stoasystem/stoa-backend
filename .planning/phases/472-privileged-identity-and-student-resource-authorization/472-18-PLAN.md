# Plan 472-18: Scope-qualified grant reconciliation and non-revival

<objective>
Close CR-02 by replacing grant-ID-only reconciliation targets with immutable full grant coordinates and proving collision-safe revocation, replay, audit recovery, and post-restore non-revival.
</objective>

## Artifacts this phase produces

- A typed immutable grant coordinate carried by every `remove_grant` reconciliation action.
- Duplicate-ID cross-scope/capability state-machine tests that exercise planning, apply, replay, audit failure, restore, and authorization.

<must_haves>
Truths: every current lineage is targeted exactly; duplicate caller IDs cannot collapse; restoration never revives quarantined authority. Artifacts: the reconciliation module and state-machine tests. Key link: the action coordinate is the same coordinate checked by `capability_repo.revoke_capability()` against the current pointer.
</must_haves>

<threat_model>
ASVS L1 assets: current capability pointers, immutable grant history, suspended privileged accounts, reconciliation checkpoints, and post-restore authority. Trust boundaries: repository inventory rows to `GrantSnapshot`; planned redacted action to exact apply target; revocation transition to later Actor grant resolution and account restore. Threats: same ID aliases two lineages, first-match selection leaves one grant live, retries revoke a replacement, audit failure loses the real mutation checkpoint, or account restore revives residual authority. Mitigations: immutable coordinate value object, exact equality resolution or direct coordinate carriage, coordinate-bound action/checkpoint identity, conditional generation/version revocation, replay-safe audit handling, current-pointer authorization, and restore/non-revival tests. Verification: same-ID/different-scope and same-ID/different-capability pairs, reversed inventory order, stale/regrant, audit failure, apply replay, and restored account Actor evaluation. Residual risk: a failed tightening run can leave the account suspended pending retry; it cannot safely restore authority. Phase 475 teacher takeover and Phase 474 fixture work remain out of scope. No provider or production mutation is needed.
</threat_model>
