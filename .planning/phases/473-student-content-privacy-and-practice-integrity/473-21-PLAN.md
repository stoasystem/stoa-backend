# Plan 473-21: Exact conversation replay and bounded AI execution

<objective>
Make fresh sends and committed retries reconstruct the same complete immutable attachment/history context and prevent incomplete extraction or stale AI work from becoming a successful response, per D-07, D-08, D-10, D-14, D-16, and D-17.

Purpose: Eliminate partial BatchGet replay, moving-history drift, prompt-marker fallbacks, and AI lease expiry races.
Output: Complete consistent reads, anchored replay, typed extraction failure, bounded AI execution, and regular/SSE fault coverage.
</objective>

## Artifacts this phase produces

- A Wave-0 compositional replay/fault suite at DynamoDB, S3 body, parser, and AI boundaries.
- Exact complete attachment/history loaders with closed row validation.
- AI invocation bounded below the lease with conditional stale-worker refusal.

<must_haves>
Truths: retries use the original complete inputs; incomplete context never reaches AI; stale AI never commits. Artifacts: read/replay helpers, typed extraction, lease/deadline guard, and transport tests. Key links: command to rows, rows to prompt, and lease to completion.
</must_haves>

<threat_model>
ASVS L1 assets are immutable student attachments, conversation history, prompt context, AI lease ownership, assistant response, and private diagnostics. Trust boundaries are BatchGet/paginated Query to typed records, S3 body/parser to prompt context, and long-running AI invocation to conditional completion. High-severity blockers are ignored UnprocessedKeys, partial/extra/reordered/foreign rows, moving history, prompt placeholders for failed private content, retryable failure frozen as 200, lease expiry during AI, stale-worker commit, or raw dependency details. Mitigate with consistent exhaustive reads, exact equality/shape validation, persisted anchors, typed all-or-nothing extraction, hard deadline/lease fencing, conditional result commit, and low-boundary regular/SSE matrices.
</threat_model>
