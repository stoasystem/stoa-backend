# Roadmap: v5.33 Controlled Expansion Execution And Revenue Validation

**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.32 Live Pilot Remediation And Reliability Fixes

## Goal

Execute controlled cohort expansion and validate revenue, support staffing, teacher operations, mobile/provider capacity, and operational scale under real usage.

## Function Purpose

- Test whether STOA can support more users without degrading support quality or learning outcomes.
- Validate billing/revenue and support operations under controlled real load.
- Produce evidence for or against public launch preparation.

## Implementation Strategy

- Expand only if v5.32 remediation gate says expansion-ready.
- Increase cohort size gradually with rollback thresholds.
- Treat billing, support staffing, teacher queue load, mobile stability, provider capacity, and BI/APM as scale gates.
- Close with public-launch-prep, hold, remediate, or rollback decision.

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 337: Live Expansion Cohort And Capacity Enablement** - Enable staged expansion cohort, staffing, flags, support coverage, and rollback thresholds.
- [ ] **Phase 338: Live Revenue Billing And Subscription Validation** - Validate payment, entitlement, refunds, failed payments, accounting handoff, and billing support.
- [ ] **Phase 339: Live Teacher Support And Customer Operations Scale** - Validate teacher-help, SLA, support CRM, lifecycle messaging, and escalation capacity.
- [ ] **Phase 340: Live Mobile Provider And Infrastructure Scale Evidence** - Validate mobile release, push, providers, BI/APM, dashboards, and infrastructure readiness under expanded use.
- [ ] **Phase 341: v5.33 Controlled Expansion Decision Gate** - Decide public-launch-prep, hold, remediate, or rollback.
