# Requirements: v8.3 Learning Outcomes Scale And AI Curriculum Improvement

**Milestone:** v8.3
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v8.2 Revenue Growth Acquisition And Paid Marketing Gate

## Purpose

Protect learning quality as rollout grows. v8.3 must convert real learning evidence into curriculum, AI, recommendation, parent clarity, and teacher workflow improvements.

## Requirements

### V8LEARN-01 Scaled Learning Outcome And Cohort Analysis

Acceptance criteria:

- Evidence covers progress, mastery, weak topics, completion, retry, support contacts, teacher help, parent report engagement, retention, and acquisition/cohort source differences.
- Learning issues are separated from billing, login, support, notification, and onboarding issues.
- Top issues are ranked by student impact, frequency, parent confusion, teacher workload, support burden, and retention impact.
- Evidence excludes raw private student content and raw prompts.

### V8LEARN-02 Curriculum Exercise Recommendation Quality Release

Acceptance criteria:

- Curriculum, exercises, explanations, sequencing, recommendations, and parent progress reporting are improved for highest-impact gaps.
- Curriculum editing remains specially authorized.
- Changed content has validation, preview, rollback metadata, analytics tags, and support/teacher notes.
- Recommendations preserve freshness, dedupe, and family-understandable rationale.

### V8LEARN-03 AI Teacher Quality Safety And Cost Release

Acceptance criteria:

- AI summaries, practice generation, explanations, teacher tools, refusal/fallback, safety, cost, latency, and provider errors are improved.
- Low-quality, unsafe, off-topic, or overconfident outputs are caught by review, refusal, or fallback behavior.
- Teacher review supports accept, edit, reject, explain, and follow-up workflows efficiently.
- AI autonomy remains reviewed or policy-bound.

### V8LEARN-04 Teacher Workload Parent Clarity And Support Reduction

Acceptance criteria:

- Teacher workload, parent confusion, repeated support contacts, unclear next steps, and repeated learning friction are reduced.
- Parent/student/teacher copy is updated where product behavior changes.
- Support macros reflect new learning behavior.
- Improvements have tests and outcome evidence.

### VERIFY-92 v8.3 Learning Scale Decision Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is learning scale, hold, remediation, content/AI freeze, or strategic pivot.
- Decision uses learning, retention, support, teacher, parent, AI/provider, curriculum, and cohort evidence.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v8.4 receives strategic risks and opportunities from learning and growth evidence.

## Out of Scope

- Fully autonomous unreviewed AI tutoring.
- Giving all teachers curriculum-edit permissions.
- Unsupported subjects/languages beyond approved rollout.
- Raw prompt or student-content evidence exposure.
