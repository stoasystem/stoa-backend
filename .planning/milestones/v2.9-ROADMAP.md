## Release Gate

v2.9 is not complete until release evidence records:

- Governance contract and approval packet template.
- Owner, approver, review cadence, break-glass, and runbook evidence.
- Backend/frontend deploy evidence, commit SHAs, timestamps, and local quality gates.
- Admin-only API request IDs and redacted payload samples.
- Production browser smoke for `/admin/report-operations`.
- Privacy denylist results proving no report artifact, S3 key, presigned URL, raw JSON/HTML, auth token, cookie, password, or AWS secret exposure.
- Final milestone audit with residual legal/compliance gaps.

## Safety Boundaries

- Do not provide legal advice or fabricate approval.
- No manual AWS console changes.
- No audit deletion or immutable object deletion.
- No customer report artifact mutation.
- No direct external support-system write.
- No broad compliance claim beyond recorded approval and verified technical behavior.
