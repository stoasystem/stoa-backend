# Roadmap: v5.6 Core Product Operations Completion

**Status:** Active planning
**Created:** 2026-07-02

Current active roadmap lives in `.planning/ROADMAP.md`.

## Goal

Complete the product-operation details required for real user usage before starting native app buildout: paid access, usage tracking, account verification, login verification codes, billing state, and admin visibility.

## Purpose

The current platform has substantial learning, billing, reporting, and dispatch foundations, but real usage still depends on lower-level operational correctness:

- A paid user must reliably receive the right entitlement.
- Quota and product usage must be durably recorded and inspectable.
- Email verification and login-code behavior must be explicit product flows.
- Parents and admins need clear billing, usage, and verification state.

## Implementation Strategy

- Audit current auth, billing, subscription, quota, usage, verification, and admin routes first.
- Build paid entitlement and usage ledger behavior before further mobile app work.
- Complete email verification and login-code policy in the backend.
- Expose customer/admin visibility for plan, payment, usage, verification, and support status.
- Keep native apps and live external provider activation as later milestones.
