# Roadmap: v5.6 Effective Entitlements And Paid Access Enforcement

**Status:** Active planning
**Created:** 2026-07-03

Current active roadmap lives in `.planning/ROADMAP.md`.

## Goal

Make paid access actually affect student usage limits through a deterministic effective-entitlement layer.

## Scope

In scope:

- Effective entitlement contract and state precedence.
- Resolver service for student/parent entitlement.
- Parent-paid linked-student quota enforcement.
- Manual override as a visible entitlement source.
- Parent/admin entitlement summary.
- Focused backend tests and release gate.

Out of scope:

- Durable usage ledger beyond entitlement summary fields.
- Email verification/login-code implementation.
- Full operations console.
- Native app implementation.
