**Status:** Active planning

**Updated:** 2026-07-02 after code-reality audit

Current active roadmap lives in `.planning/ROADMAP.md`.

## Goal

Complete the real-user operational layer before native app buildout: paid entitlement, usage ledger, account/email verification, login-code policy, customer billing state, and admin support visibility.

## Current Reality

The codebase has Cognito password auth, parent subscription/billing routes, Stripe checkout/webhooks, billing events, admin billing views, and daily quota counters.

The missing layer is product correctness: parent paid billing must grant linked student access, usage needs a durable ledger, email verification must be real, login-code policy must be explicit, and support/admin views need entitlement + usage + verification state.
