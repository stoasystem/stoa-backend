# Requirements: v5.36 Live Pilot Operations Feedback And Product Fixes

**Milestone:** v5.36
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.35 Real Pilot Blocker Burn-Down And Launch Execution

## Purpose

Operate the approved live pilot and convert real user evidence into product fixes before any expansion.

## Requirements

### PILOTOPS-01 First Cohort Live Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- v5.35 start gate is `start_limited_pilot`.
- Approved cohort accounts are enabled under documented feature flags and rollback controls.
- Activation, login, entitlement, first learning action, mobile install, notification, and support intake are tracked.
- User-facing communications and support coverage are active for the pilot window.

### PILOTOPS-02 Daily Feedback Incident And Metrics Review

Acceptance criteria:

- Daily review covers activation, learning actions, teacher response, support SLA, billing/entitlement, notification delivery, mobile stability, AI/provider quality, and incidents.
- Evidence includes redacted request IDs, timestamps, owners, severity, and current disposition.
- Any start-blocking issue immediately triggers pause or rollback review.
- Metrics distinguish real usage from test/dry-run traffic.

### PILOTOPS-03 High-Impact Pilot Fix Implementation

Acceptance criteria:

- Pilot findings are ranked by severity, frequency, learning impact, support load, and revenue impact.
- High-impact fixes include focused tests, rollback notes, and release evidence.
- Support copy and operator runbooks are updated for changed behavior.
- Fixes do not widen cohort scope without explicit gate approval.

### PILOTOPS-04 Pilot Learning Outcome And Support Quality Review

Acceptance criteria:

- Learning progress, exercise completion, teacher response quality, AI summary/exercise quality, and parent satisfaction are reviewed.
- Known weak spots in curriculum, AI output, teacher dispatch, or practice generation are converted into v5.38 candidate work.
- Support volume and resolution quality are reviewed against capacity.
- Unresolved issues have owners and next action.

### VERIFY-70 Pilot Outcome Decision Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is expand, hold, rollback, or continue remediation.
- Expansion is not recommended unless activation, learning, support, mobile, billing, and incident signals meet documented thresholds.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.37/v5.38/v5.39 handoff reflects real evidence, not planned assumptions.

## Out of Scope

- Public launch.
- Paid marketing.
- Broad cohort expansion before the decision gate.
- New product areas not observed in pilot evidence.
