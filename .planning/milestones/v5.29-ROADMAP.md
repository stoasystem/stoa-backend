# Roadmap: v5.29 Public Launch Readiness Growth And Self-Serve Onboarding

**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.28 Controlled Expansion Revenue And Operations Scale

## Goal

Prepare STOA for public launch or a larger market-facing release by completing self-serve onboarding, growth loops, pricing/package readiness, public support operations, app-store readiness, and final launch go/no-go controls.

## Function Purpose

- Make the product usable beyond manually managed cohorts.
- Prepare acquisition, onboarding, payment, support, and retention operations for broader traffic.
- Force a final launch decision based on evidence, not optimism.

## Implementation Strategy

- Start only if v5.28 expansion evidence supports broader launch preparation.
- Build self-serve account/subscription/onboarding controls with clear support fallback.
- Add growth and lifecycle mechanics that do not compromise learning quality or support capacity.
- Keep public launch behind final go/no-go, rollback, support, and provider-readiness gates.

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 317: Self-Serve Onboarding And Account Conversion** - Build self-serve signup, verification, parent/student setup, entitlement, mobile install, and first-learning-action conversion flow.
- [ ] **Phase 318: Pricing Packaging Growth And Lifecycle Readiness** - Prepare pricing/package display, conversion events, referral/waitlist/lifecycle loops, and retention reporting.
- [ ] **Phase 319: Public Support Knowledge Base And Launch Communications** - Add support docs, onboarding communications, escalation paths, release notes, and public-facing issue handling.
- [ ] **Phase 320: App Store Public Release And Production Launch Controls** - Prepare app-store/public release assets, production flags, provider capacity, launch dashboard, freeze, rollback, and incident staffing.
- [ ] **Phase 321: v5.29 Public Launch Readiness Gate** - Decide public launch, continued controlled expansion, hold, or further hardening.
