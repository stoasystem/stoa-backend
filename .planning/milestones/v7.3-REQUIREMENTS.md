# Requirements: v7.3 Public Launch Execution Or Hold Decision

**Milestone:** v7.3
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v7.2 Public Launch Preparation And Acquisition Readiness

## Purpose

Execute public launch only if final approval and healthy launch-prep evidence exist. Otherwise keep STOA in controlled expansion, hold, rollback, or remediation.

## Requirements

### V7LAUNCH-01 Final Launch Approval And Freeze Execution

Acceptance criteria:

- Final launch approval, rollout scope, release freeze, release artifacts, support staffing, teacher capacity, provider readiness, mobile readiness, and rollback authority are confirmed.
- Public launch and paid marketing approvals are recorded separately.
- Known limitations and disabled features are visible to users/support.
- Go/no-go owners are identified.

### V7LAUNCH-02 Staged Launch Enablement And Production Smoke

Acceptance criteria:

- Staged rollout or continued controlled expansion is enabled according to approved scope.
- Production smoke covers login, verification, onboarding, entitlement, usage, notification, support, mobile, learning, AI/help, revenue, and rollback.
- Mutations are approved, scoped, reversible, and recorded.
- Evidence includes request IDs, build/deploy IDs, timestamps, owner signoff, and blocker states.

### V7LAUNCH-03 Launch Room Support Revenue Learning Monitoring

Acceptance criteria:

- Launch-room monitoring covers auth, billing, usage, notifications, support SLA, teacher operations, learning quality, AI/provider, mobile, incidents, revenue, retention, and acquisition intake.
- Daily review distinguishes real users from internal/test traffic.
- Severe issues trigger pause, rollback, hotfix, or provider disablement review.
- Support and incident communications are active.

### V7LAUNCH-04 Launch Incident Remediation And User Communication

Acceptance criteria:

- Hotfixes, support actions, incident communications, rollback, disablement, and user copy are executed when needed.
- Release evidence and support macros are updated for any changed behavior.
- User-visible limitations are not hidden.
- Residual risks are owner-assigned with next action.

### VERIFY-87 v7.3 Launch Outcome Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is launched, continue controlled expansion, hold, rollback, or remediation.
- Decision uses launch-room, customer, revenue, learning, support, mobile, provider, incident, acquisition, and release evidence.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v7.4 scope depends on launched or controlled-expansion outcome.

## Out of Scope

- Paid marketing without separate approval.
- Broad launch beyond approved rollout scope.
- Unreviewed AI autonomy.
- Hiding known limitations.
