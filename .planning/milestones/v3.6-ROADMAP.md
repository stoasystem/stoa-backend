# Roadmap: v3.6 Full WebSocket Realtime Notifications

**Status:** Active
**Started:** 2026-06-08

## Goal

Turn the v3.5 in-product notification foundation into full WebSocket realtime notifications for core learning and operations workflows.

## Planned Phases

| Phase | Name | Status | Requirement |
|-------|------|--------|-------------|
| 112 | Full WebSocket Transport Contract And Infra Readiness | Planned | WS-01 |
| 113 | Backend WebSocket Connection And Event Delivery | Planned | WS-02 |
| 114 | Realtime Notification Client And UX | Planned | UI-21 |
| 115 | v3.6 Functional Release Gate And Realtime Audit | Planned | VERIFY-19 |

## Release Gate

v3.6 is complete when:

- WebSocket transport contract and infrastructure readiness are documented.
- Backend authenticated connection, subscription, fanout, and cleanup flows work.
- Frontend realtime client works with reconnect and fallback behavior.
- Focused backend/frontend checks and browser smoke prove the workflow works.
- `STOA_DOCS_FEATURE_GAP_AUDIT.md` records the full WebSocket outcome and residual push/email/native notification scope.
