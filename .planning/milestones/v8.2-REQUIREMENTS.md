# Requirements: v8.2 Revenue Growth Acquisition And Paid Marketing Gate

**Milestone:** v8.2
**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v8.1 Live Rollout Operations And Incident Remediation

## Purpose

Evaluate and execute growth only when rollout evidence supports it. v8.2 must keep paid marketing separately approved and capacity-gated.

## Requirements

### V8GROWTH-01 Revenue Conversion Retention Evidence Review

Acceptance criteria:

- Conversion, retention, churn, refunds, failed payments, usage/quota, pricing, lifecycle, support corrections, and revenue reconciliation are reviewed.
- Revenue evidence distinguishes real customers from internal/test accounts.
- Parent copy and support copy match actual product behavior.
- Revenue risks have owner, mitigation, and decision status.

### V8GROWTH-02 Acquisition Referral Waitlist Quality Review

Acceptance criteria:

- Referral, waitlist, invite, acquisition source quality, cohort fit, and fraud/abuse signals are reviewed.
- Intake is capped by support, teacher, billing, mobile, provider, incident, and learning quality capacity.
- Acquisition reporting separates organic, referral, waitlist, partner, internal, and paid sources.
- Poor-fit or high-risk intake paths are blocked or remediated.

### V8GROWTH-03 Growth Support Capacity And Lifecycle Fixes

Acceptance criteria:

- Support capacity, lifecycle messaging, billing support, onboarding, activation, reminder, renewal, failed payment, cancellation, and win-back gaps are fixed or deferred.
- Preferences, locale, opt-out, failed delivery, refund, and escalation paths are handled.
- Support staffing and teacher capacity are aligned with growth limits.
- Fixes have tests, release evidence, and support-visible copy.

### V8GROWTH-04 Paid Marketing Approval And Experiment Design Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Paid marketing experiments, if proposed, have owner approval, budget, target audience, capacity gate, success metrics, stop criteria, and rollback/disable controls.
- Paid spend remains blocked if support, revenue, learning quality, incident, mobile, or provider evidence is unhealthy.
- Marketing copy does not hide known limitations or disabled features.
- Experiment evidence is auditable.

### VERIFY-91 v8.2 Growth Decision Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is growth scale, organic-only growth, paid marketing prep, hold, rollback, or remediation.
- Decision uses revenue, retention, acquisition, support, teacher, learning, mobile, provider, incident, and release evidence.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v8.3 receives learning-quality risks from growth evidence.

## Out of Scope

- Unbounded paid marketing.
- Unsupported market/language expansion.
- Enterprise sales automation unless separately approved.
- Hiding known limitations.
