# Roadmap: v5.38 Learning Outcomes Curriculum And AI Quality Scale

**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.37 Revenue Conversion And Self-Serve Growth Completion

## Goal

Improve the core learning product after real usage: curriculum coverage, exercise quality, adaptive recommendations, AI teacher drafts, automatic summaries, practice generation, and learning outcome reporting.

## Function Purpose

- Make STOA visibly better at helping students learn, not merely complete operational workflows.
- Convert pilot learning evidence into curriculum fixes, AI evaluation data, and teacher-reviewed automation improvements.
- Give parents, teachers, and admins a trustworthy view of progress and next steps.

## Implementation Strategy

- Use pilot evidence, teacher review, curriculum analytics, and support tickets to choose content/AI work.
- Keep teacher-reviewed AI and special authorization boundaries in place.
- Prioritize measurable student progress, exercise completion, retention, and reduced support confusion.
- Close with learning-quality release evidence and a scale recommendation.

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 362: Learning Outcome Evidence And Gap Analysis** - Analyze real learning progress, weak topics, completion, parent feedback, and teacher/support notes.
- [ ] **Phase 363: Curriculum Coverage And Exercise Bank Completion** - Fill the highest-value curriculum and practice gaps discovered in real usage.
- [ ] **Phase 364: AI Summary Exercise And Teacher Tool Quality Improvements** - Improve AI summaries, explanations, exercise drafts, and teacher review tools with eval coverage.
- [ ] **Phase 365: Adaptive Recommendation And Assignment Quality Loop** - Tighten recommendations, assignment automation, freshness, dedupe, and family explanations.
- [ ] **Phase 366: Learning Quality Scale Gate** - Decide whether learning quality supports expansion, needs remediation, or requires content/AI hold.
