# Requirements: v7.1 Controlled Expansion Operations And Remediation

**Milestone:** v7.1
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v7.0 Final Live Approval And Controlled Expansion Start

## Purpose

Operate the controlled expansion cohort and remediate real bottlenecks before any public launch preparation.

## Requirements

### V7EXPAND-01 Controlled Expansion Day-One Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- Cohort enablement, support coverage, teacher coverage, dashboards, incident checks, pause criteria, and rollback controls are active.
- Daily evidence distinguishes real users from internal, fixture, dry-run, and test traffic.
- Support, teacher, finance, mobile, provider, and incident owners review signals.
- Any severe issue triggers pause, rollback, or hotfix review.

### V7EXPAND-02 Expansion Account Revenue Support Fixes

Acceptance criteria:

- Account, verification, entitlement, subscription, usage/quota, billing, lifecycle, support, and parent clarity issues are fixed or explicitly deferred.
- Revenue-impacting fixes are auditable, reversible, and support-visible.
- Support macros and parent copy are updated for changed behavior.
- Regression coverage exists for high-impact fixes.

### V7EXPAND-03 Expansion Learning Mobile Teacher Provider Fixes

Acceptance criteria:

- Learning, AI/provider, teacher dispatch, mobile, notification, curriculum, recommendation, and parent progress issues are fixed or explicitly deferred.
- Curriculum changes remain specially authorized.
- AI behavior remains reviewed or policy-bound.
- Mobile/provider fallbacks are visible to support and users when needed.

### V7EXPAND-04 Expansion Reliability Release Evidence

Acceptance criteria:

- Fixes include tests, smoke evidence, release notes, rollback notes, and operator runbook updates.
- Release evidence links code SHA, deploy/build IDs, request IDs where applicable, timestamp, and owner.
- Dashboards and alerts distinguish test, controlled expansion, and real customer traffic.
- Known residual risks are owner-assigned.

### VERIFY-85 v7.1 Expansion Outcome Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is public-launch-prep, continue expansion, hold, rollback, or remediation.
- Decision uses activation, support, teacher, revenue, learning, mobile, provider, incident, and release evidence.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v7.2 is allowed only if expansion evidence supports launch preparation.

## Out of Scope

- Public launch execution.
- Paid marketing execution.
- Uncontrolled provider writes.
- Broad cohort expansion without gate approval.
