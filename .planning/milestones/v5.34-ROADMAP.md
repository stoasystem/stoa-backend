# Roadmap: v5.34 Public Launch Execution And Post-Launch Operations

**Status:** Contract Complete
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.33 Controlled Expansion Execution And Revenue Validation

## Goal

Execute a public launch or continue controlled expansion based on final approval, then operate post-launch monitoring, support, revenue, incident response, and learning loops.

## Function Purpose

- Move from launch readiness into launch execution only if final approval exists.
- Ensure self-serve onboarding, growth, support, app-store, provider, and rollback operations are live-ready.
- Establish post-launch operating cadence and expansion/hold criteria.

## Implementation Strategy

- Start only if v5.33 supports public-launch preparation and final approval is granted.
- Use staged rollout with freeze, rollback, support staffing, and dashboard ownership.
- Treat post-launch issues as structured product evidence.
- Close with launch outcome report and v5.35 recommendation.
