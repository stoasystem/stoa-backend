# Requirements: v5.37 Revenue Conversion And Self-Serve Growth Completion

**Milestone:** v5.37
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.36 Live Pilot Operations Feedback And Product Fixes

## Purpose

Finish revenue conversion and self-serve growth capability after pilot evidence shows what parents actually need to buy, renew, understand usage, and get billing help.

## Requirements

### REVCONV-01 Pricing Package And Checkout Reality Closeout

Acceptance criteria:

- Parent-facing package, trial, upgrade, downgrade, renewal, cancellation, failed payment, and invoice states are complete for the approved market scope.
- Checkout supports approved payment methods and records provider/request evidence without secrets or raw provider payloads.
- Paywall and entitlement state match account status immediately enough for real use.
- Edge cases have clear user copy and support explanations.

### REVCONV-02 Entitlement Revenue And Usage Reconciliation

Acceptance criteria:

- Billing provider state, entitlement state, usage ledger, quota display, invoices/refunds, and admin support view reconcile for pilot accounts.
- Reconciliation jobs or reports surface drift, stale entitlements, duplicate events, missing usage, and manual overrides.
- Parent/admin explanations avoid private student content while making limits and access understandable.
- Revenue-impacting corrections are auditable and owner-approved.

### REVCONV-03 Lifecycle Messaging And Retention Loop Completion

Acceptance criteria:

- Onboarding, activation, reminder, renewal, failed payment, trial ending, cancellation, and win-back messages exist for approved channels.
- Message sending respects consent, preferences, locale, quiet hours where applicable, and support capacity.
- Support/admin can see message status and reason without raw provider internals.
- Lifecycle metrics distinguish sent, delivered, failed, clicked, converted, unsubscribed, and support-contacted states.

### REVCONV-04 Referral Waitlist And Controlled Growth Surface

Acceptance criteria:

- Referral, waitlist, invite, or controlled share flows are implemented behind feature and capacity gates.
- Growth surfaces explain availability, eligibility, and next steps clearly.
- Fraud/abuse handling is bounded enough for internal development stage.
- Growth intake feeds support and cohort planning rather than opening uncontrolled public launch.

### VERIFY-71 Revenue Growth Readiness Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is controlled growth, paid-marketing prep, hold, or revenue remediation.
- Decision uses conversion, support load, billing drift, retention, and user satisfaction evidence.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- Public paid marketing remains gated until support and revenue operations are ready.

## Out of Scope

- Enterprise sales automation.
- Unbounded paid marketing.
- Multi-country tax/accounting expansion beyond approved launch market.
- Hiding billing or quota limits from parents.
