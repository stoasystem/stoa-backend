# Requirements: v5.33 Controlled Expansion Execution And Revenue Validation

**Milestone:** v5.33
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.32 Live Pilot Remediation And Reliability Fixes

## Purpose

Expand beyond the initial pilot only if remediation evidence supports it. v5.33 validates real revenue operations, support/teacher capacity, mobile/provider readiness, and operational scale under a larger controlled cohort.

## Requirements

### LIVEEXPAND-01 Live Expansion Cohort And Capacity Enablement

Acceptance criteria:

- Expanded cohort size, user mix, rollout stages, rollback thresholds, and excluded features are approved.
- Capacity assumptions cover API, mobile, providers, AI, teacher-help, support, billing, BI/APM, and incident response.
- Cohort communication and consent updates are sent through approved channels.
- Expansion does not start while unresolved v5.32 blockers remain.

### LIVEEXPAND-02 Live Revenue Billing And Subscription Validation

Acceptance criteria:

- Payment, entitlement, refunds, failed payments, dunning, accounting handoff, and billing support flows are verified under expanded use.
- Billing support views expose provider state without raw provider payloads or secrets.
- Finance/support ownership and reconciliation cadence are documented.
- Revenue metrics are included in expansion dashboard.

### LIVEEXPAND-03 Live Teacher Support And Customer Operations Scale

Acceptance criteria:

- Teacher-help queue, SLA, dispatch, escalation, support CRM, lifecycle messaging, and feedback workflows are validated against expansion assumptions.
- Staffing plan covers normal hours, spike handling, and escalation ownership.
- Parent/student support copy is accurate for expansion scope.
- Support load and teacher response quality are measured.

### LIVEEXPAND-04 Live Mobile Provider And Infrastructure Scale Evidence

Acceptance criteria:

- Mobile release channel, push delivery, offline/cache behavior, auth/session, and crash/performance signals are checked under expanded use.
- Provider capacity and BI/APM dashboards are reviewed.
- Infrastructure runbooks cover scaling, rollback, and provider degradation.
- Evidence remains redacted and support-safe.

### VERIFY-67 Controlled Expansion Decision Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is public-launch-prep, hold, remediate, or rollback.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.34 scope is adjusted based on expansion outcomes.
- Public launch remains blocked unless expansion metrics and support capacity meet thresholds.

## Out of Scope

- Unbounded public signup.
- Paid marketing execution.
- Enterprise sales automation.
- Ignoring support/teacher capacity thresholds.
- Bypassing billing/provider rollout controls.
