## Release Gate

v3.5 is complete when:

- Notification event and teacher assistance contracts are documented.
- Backend notification events and summary seeds work without full WebSocket infrastructure.
- Tutor/admin UI uses real backend event/summary data.
- Focused backend/frontend checks and browser smoke prove the workflow works.
- `STOA_DOCS_FEATURE_GAP_AUDIT.md` records the notification/teacher-assistance outcome and residual Phase 2 scope.
