# Roadmap: v7.4 Post-Launch Scale Customer Success And Growth Governance

**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v7.3 Public Launch Execution Or Hold Decision

## Goal

Operate the post-launch or post-expansion system at scale. v7.4 focuses on customer success, support operations, revenue reconciliation, learning quality, growth governance, mobile/provider reliability, incident review, and roadmap feedback.

## Function Purpose

- Convert launch/expansion evidence into sustainable operations.
- Protect learning quality and support quality while growth increases.
- Decide whether to scale growth, hold, remediate, or start the next strategic version.

## Implementation Strategy

- Use v7.3 outcome as the operating mode: launched, controlled expansion, hold, rollback, or remediation.
- Track customer success, revenue, retention, learning outcomes, support load, teacher load, mobile reliability, provider health, incidents, and acquisition quality.
- Keep paid marketing spend gated by support/revenue/learning capacity.
- Close with scale, hold, rollback, remediation, or v8 recommendation.

## Phase Details

### Phase 442: Post-Launch Customer Success And Support Operations

**Goal**: Operate support, success, onboarding, lifecycle, incident communications, and parent/student/teacher feedback loops.
**Depends on**: v7.3 Public Launch Execution Or Hold Decision.
**Requirements**: V7SCALE-01
**Success Criteria**:

1. Customer success, support, onboarding, lifecycle, incident communications, and feedback loops are active.
2. Support load, response time, resolution quality, teacher workload, parent satisfaction, and student friction are tracked.
3. Known limitations and disabled features remain visible.
4. Escalation paths cover billing, login, learning, mobile, AI/provider, notification, and refund issues.

### Phase 443: Revenue Retention Growth Governance

**Goal**: Reconcile revenue, retention, churn, pricing, lifecycle, referral/waitlist, acquisition quality, and paid marketing gate evidence.
**Depends on**: Phase 442.
**Requirements**: V7SCALE-02
**Success Criteria**:

1. Revenue, retention, churn, refunds, failed payments, usage/quota, pricing, lifecycle, and support corrections are reconciled.
2. Referral/waitlist/acquisition quality and capacity gates are reviewed.
3. Paid marketing remains gated by support, revenue, retention, learning quality, and incident readiness.
4. Growth decisions are auditable and owner-approved.

### Phase 444: Learning Quality Mobile Provider Reliability Review

**Goal**: Review learning outcomes, AI/curriculum quality, teacher workload, mobile reliability, provider health, and support burden.
**Depends on**: Phase 443.
**Requirements**: V7SCALE-03
**Success Criteria**:

1. Learning outcomes, curriculum quality, AI teacher quality, recommendations, parent progress reporting, teacher workload, mobile reliability, provider health, and support burden are reviewed.
2. Curriculum editing remains specially authorized.
3. AI autonomy remains reviewed or policy-bound.
4. Quality issues are converted into product, content, AI, support, or accepted-risk actions.

### Phase 445: Scale Incident Release And Roadmap Feedback Loop

**Goal**: Review incidents, releases, dashboards, rollback, data quality, support load, and roadmap feedback.
**Depends on**: Phase 444.
**Requirements**: V7SCALE-04
**Success Criteria**:

1. Incidents, releases, dashboards, alerts, rollback, migrations, data quality, support load, and roadmap feedback are reviewed.
2. Issues have owner, severity, user impact, mitigation, and next action.
3. Release and incident evidence link code SHA, deploy/build IDs, request IDs where applicable, timestamp, and owner.
4. Roadmap feedback separates customer value, growth, learning quality, operations, and technical debt.

### Phase 446: Scale Or Next Strategy Gate

**Goal**: Decide scale growth, hold, rollback, remediation, or next strategic version.
**Depends on**: Phase 445.
**Requirements**: VERIFY-88
**Success Criteria**:

1. Decision is scale growth, hold, rollback, remediation, or next strategic version.
2. Decision uses customer success, revenue, retention, learning, support, mobile, provider, incident, release, and acquisition evidence.
3. Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
4. v8 recommendation, if any, is based on live risks and opportunities.
