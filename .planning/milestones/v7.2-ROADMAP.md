# Roadmap: v7.2 Public Launch Preparation And Acquisition Readiness

**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v7.1 Controlled Expansion Operations And Remediation

## Goal

Prepare public launch only if controlled expansion evidence is healthy. v7.2 covers launch scope, pricing/package, web/mobile/app-store readiness, support staffing, lifecycle messaging, acquisition limits, waitlist/referral intake, provider readiness, and final owner approvals.

## Function Purpose

- Convert controlled expansion evidence into a public launch preparation package.
- Keep paid marketing separately gated.
- Ensure support, billing, learning quality, provider, mobile, and incident readiness can absorb public traffic.

## Implementation Strategy

- Use v7.1 evidence as the launch-readiness input.
- Prepare staged rollout, copy, support macros, lifecycle messages, dashboards, freeze, rollback, and capacity gates.
- Review paid marketing separately from organic/waitlist/referral launch readiness.
- Close with launch-ready, controlled-expansion-only, hold, rollback, or remediation.

## Phase Details

### Phase 432: Launch Scope Pricing Package And Copy Readiness

**Goal**: Finalize audience, package, pricing, copy, disabled features, known limitations, and user/support communications.
**Depends on**: v7.1 Controlled Expansion Operations And Remediation.
**Requirements**: V7LAUNCHPREP-01
**Success Criteria**:

1. Audience, rollout scope, pricing/package, plan limits, disabled features, known limitations, eligibility, and support policy are final.
2. Parent/student/teacher/admin copy is ready for included and excluded features.
3. Pricing and billing support copy match actual entitlement and usage behavior.
4. Paid marketing approval is tracked separately from launch prep.

### Phase 433: Web Mobile App Store And Provider Launch Readiness

**Goal**: Verify frontend/web, mobile/app-store, backend, payment, notification, support, BI/APM, AI/provider, and monitoring readiness.
**Depends on**: Phase 432.
**Requirements**: V7LAUNCHPREP-02
**Success Criteria**:

1. Frontend/web, mobile/app-store, backend, payment, notification, support, BI/APM, AI/provider, and monitoring readiness are verified.
2. App-store constraints and mobile limitations are explicit.
3. Provider failures have disablement and fallback controls.
4. Release evidence links code SHA, deploy/build IDs, request IDs where applicable, timestamp, and owner.

### Phase 434: Support Lifecycle Acquisition And Capacity Readiness

**Goal**: Prepare support staffing, lifecycle messages, referral/waitlist, acquisition limits, retention reporting, and support escalation.
**Depends on**: Phase 433.
**Requirements**: V7LAUNCHPREP-03
**Success Criteria**:

1. Support staffing, incident coverage, lifecycle messages, referral/waitlist intake, acquisition limits, retention reporting, and escalation paths are ready.
2. Growth intake is capped by support, teacher, billing, mobile, provider, and incident capacity.
3. Support macros cover billing, login, learning, mobile, AI/provider, notification, and refund issues.
4. Preferences, locale, delivery failure, and opt-out behavior are handled.

### Phase 435: Launch Freeze Rollback Dashboard And Evidence Package

**Goal**: Prepare release freeze, rollback, dashboards, incident runbooks, owner approvals, and evidence package.
**Depends on**: Phase 434.
**Requirements**: V7LAUNCHPREP-04
**Success Criteria**:

1. Launch freeze, staged rollout, rollback, dashboards, alerts, incident runbooks, owner approvals, and evidence package are current.
2. Dashboards cover auth, entitlement, usage, billing, notification, support, teacher, AI/provider, mobile, learning, incidents, revenue, and retention.
3. Known residual risks have owner, mitigation, and go/no-go status.
4. Evidence excludes secrets, raw prompts, raw student content, private object keys, and raw provider payloads.

### Phase 436: Launch Preparation Gate

**Goal**: Decide launch-ready, controlled-expansion-only, hold, rollback, or remediation.
**Depends on**: Phase 435.
**Requirements**: VERIFY-86
**Success Criteria**:

1. Decision is launch-ready, controlled-expansion-only, hold, rollback, or remediation.
2. Decision uses expansion, revenue, learning, support, mobile, provider, incident, acquisition, and release evidence.
3. Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
4. v7.3 proceeds only with launch-ready evidence and final owner approval.
