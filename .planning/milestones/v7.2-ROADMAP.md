# Roadmap: v7.2 Public Launch Preparation And Acquisition Readiness

**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v7.1 Controlled Expansion Operations And Remediation

## Goal

Prepare public launch only if controlled expansion evidence is healthy. v7.2 covers launch scope, pricing/package, web/mobile/app-store readiness, support staffing, lifecycle messaging, acquisition limits, waitlist/referral intake, provider readiness, and final owner approvals.

## Function Purpose

- Convert controlled expansion evidence into a public launch preparation package.
- Keep paid marketing separately gated.
- Ensure support, billing, learning quality, provider, mobile, and incident readiness can absorb public traffic.

## Implementation Strategy

- Use v7.1 evidence as the launch-readiness input.
- Prepare staged rollout, copy, support macros, lifecycle messages, dashboards, freeze, rollback, and capacity gates.
- Review paid marketing separately from organic/waitlist/referral launch readiness.
- Close with launch-ready, controlled-expansion-only, hold, rollback, or remediation.

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 432: Launch Scope Pricing Package And Copy Readiness** - Finalize audience, package, pricing, copy, disabled features, known limitations, and user/support communications.
- [ ] **Phase 433: Web Mobile App Store And Provider Launch Readiness** - Verify frontend/web, mobile/app-store, backend, payment, notification, support, BI/APM, AI/provider, and monitoring readiness.
- [ ] **Phase 434: Support Lifecycle Acquisition And Capacity Readiness** - Prepare support staffing, lifecycle messages, referral/waitlist, acquisition limits, retention reporting, and support escalation.
- [ ] **Phase 435: Launch Freeze Rollback Dashboard And Evidence Package** - Prepare release freeze, rollback, dashboards, incident runbooks, owner approvals, and evidence package.
- [ ] **Phase 436: v7.2 Launch Preparation Gate** - Decide launch-ready, controlled-expansion-only, hold, rollback, or remediation.
