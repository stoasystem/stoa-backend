## Goal

Fix the highest-impact issues from the real limited pilot across activation, mobile, billing, learning quality, AI, support, and reliability before expansion.

## Function Purpose

- Turn live user evidence into targeted product improvements.

- Keep fixes focused; defer speculative polish.
- Close with expansion-ready, continue-pilot, or another-remediation decision.
