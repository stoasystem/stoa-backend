# Requirements: v5.38 Learning Outcomes Curriculum And AI Quality Scale

**Milestone:** v5.38
**Status:** Contract Complete
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.37 Revenue Conversion And Self-Serve Growth Completion

## Purpose

Make the learning product stronger using real pilot/growth evidence. v5.38 focuses on curriculum, exercises, adaptive recommendations, AI teacher tools, summaries, practice generation, and learning outcome reporting.

## Requirements

### LEARNQUAL-01 Learning Outcome Evidence And Gap Analysis

Acceptance criteria:

- Real usage evidence identifies weak topics, drop-off points, confusing exercises, teacher intervention points, AI quality issues, and parent misunderstandings.
- Evidence distinguishes product issues from onboarding/support issues.
- Learning metrics include completion, retry, mastery/progress, teacher help, AI draft review, parent report engagement, and support contacts.
- Priority list is ranked by student impact, frequency, severity, and implementation effort.

### LEARNQUAL-02 Curriculum Coverage And Exercise Bank Completion

Acceptance criteria:

- Highest-priority curriculum gaps are filled with reviewed lessons, exercises, explanations, and metadata.
- Content migration/editor authorization remains restricted to specially authorized operators.
- Exercise bank changes have validation, preview, rollback metadata, and analytics tags.
- Parent/student views expose updated content without broken sequencing.

### LEARNQUAL-03 AI Summary Exercise And Teacher Tool Quality Improvements

Acceptance criteria:

- AI summaries, explanations, exercise drafts, and teacher-assist outputs have updated evaluation fixtures.
- Low-quality, unsafe, off-topic, or overconfident outputs are caught by review gates or downgraded to human review.
- Teacher workflow makes accepting, editing, rejecting, and explaining AI drafts efficient.
- Cost, latency, refusal, and fallback states remain observable.

### LEARNQUAL-04 Adaptive Recommendation And Assignment Quality Loop

Acceptance criteria:

- Recommendations use recent learning, completed assignments, weak topics, content availability, and freshness.
- Assignment automation remains reviewed or policy-bound and avoids duplicate/stale work.
- Family explanations are understandable without exposing private model prompts or internal scoring.
- Teacher/admin can correct recommendations and see why they were produced.

### VERIFY-72 Learning Quality Scale Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is expand learning scope, remediate content/AI, hold automation, or continue targeted fixes.
- Learning quality evidence is recorded without raw private student content.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.39 reliability/operations handoff includes remaining learning-quality risks.

## Out of Scope

- Fully autonomous unreviewed AI teaching.
- Giving all teachers curriculum-edit permissions.
- Unsupported subjects or languages beyond approved rollout.
- Raw prompt/student-content exposure in evidence.
