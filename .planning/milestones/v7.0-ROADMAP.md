# Roadmap: v7.0 Final Live Approval And Controlled Expansion Start

**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v6.9 Public Launch Decision And Market Readiness

## Goal

Turn v6.9 market-readiness contracts into a real controlled-expansion start or an explicit hold. v7.0 must gather final owner approval, current production evidence, provider/mobile/support readiness, cohort scope, and rollback controls before enabling a larger controlled cohort.

## Reality Check

v6.9 is complete as a market-readiness decision contract. It does not approve public launch, paid marketing, uncontrolled provider writes, or broad expansion by itself. Public launch prep is possible only with healthy evidence and final owner approval; paid marketing remains separately gated.

The next work should therefore start v7, but v7.0 must be controlled expansion execution, not a public-launch claim.

## Function Purpose

- Convert v6.9 launch/expansion decision inputs into final real approval.
- Start a larger controlled cohort only if current evidence is healthy.
- Keep broad public launch and paid marketing blocked unless later milestones explicitly approve them.
- Preserve rollback, pause, support staffing, provider disablement, and user-visible limitation controls.

## Implementation Strategy

- Refresh final owner approval, scope, support capacity, provider/mobile state, revenue reconciliation, learning quality, and incident readiness.
- Run production checks read-only by default; allow only explicitly approved, scoped, reversible pilot/expansion actions.
- Record redacted evidence with timestamps, owners, request/build IDs, rollout flags, cohort aliases, blocker states, and rollback authority.
- Close with controlled expansion start, hold, rollback, or another remediation cycle.

## Phase Details

### Phase 422: Final Owner Approval And Expansion Scope Refresh

**Goal**: Confirm decision owner, rollout scope, cohort cap, disabled features, pricing posture, support coverage, and risk acceptance.
**Depends on**: v6.9 Public Launch Decision And Market Readiness.
**Requirements**: V7APPROVAL-01
**Success Criteria**:

1. Final decision owner, product owner, support owner, teacher owner, finance owner, mobile owner, provider owners, and incident owner are recorded.
2. Rollout scope, cohort cap, disabled features, pricing posture, support hours, teacher coverage, risk acceptance, and rollback authority are approved.
3. Public launch and paid marketing approval are explicitly separate from controlled expansion approval.
4. Known limitations are visible to users/support where relevant.

### Phase 423: Production Provider Mobile Support Evidence Refresh

**Goal**: Refresh payment, notification, support CRM, BI/APM, AI/provider, mobile/app-store, monitoring, restore/tabletop, and incident readiness.
**Depends on**: Phase 422.
**Requirements**: V7APPROVAL-02
**Success Criteria**:

1. Payment, notification, support CRM, BI/APM, AI/provider, mobile/app-store, monitoring, restore/tabletop, and incident readiness are refreshed.
2. Evidence includes timestamp, owner, environment, request/build ID where applicable, result, blocker state, fallback, and rollback/disable control.
3. Missing dependencies are classified as accepted, disabled for expansion, hold-blocking, or not required.
4. Evidence excludes secrets, auth tokens, verification codes, raw provider payloads, raw student content, private object keys, and presigned URLs.

### Phase 424: Controlled Expansion Cohort And Rollout Setup

**Goal**: Prepare cohort aliases, flags, communications, dashboards, support staffing, teacher coverage, pause criteria, and rollback.
**Depends on**: Phase 423.
**Requirements**: V7APPROVAL-03
**Success Criteria**:

1. Cohort aliases, eligibility, consent state, communications, feature flags, dashboards, support staffing, teacher coverage, pause criteria, and rollback plan are prepared.
2. Growth intake is capped and support-visible.
3. Parent/student/teacher/admin copy explains included and disabled features.
4. Rollout setup distinguishes production, dry-run, test, and internal accounts.

### Phase 425: Expansion Start Smoke And Day-Zero Verification

**Goal**: Run approved production smoke for account, entitlement, usage, notification, support, mobile, learning, and revenue paths.
**Depends on**: Phase 424.
**Requirements**: V7APPROVAL-04
**Success Criteria**:

1. Approved smoke covers login, verification, onboarding, entitlement, usage ledger, quota, notification, support, mobile, learning, AI/help, and revenue paths.
2. Any production mutation is approved, scoped, reversible, and tied to pilot/expansion accounts.
3. Request IDs, account aliases, build IDs, timestamps, blocker states, and owner signoff are recorded.
4. Start blockers are converted into owner/action rows.

### Phase 426: Controlled Expansion Start Gate

**Goal**: Decide start controlled expansion, hold, rollback, or remediate.
**Depends on**: Phase 425.
**Requirements**: VERIFY-84
**Success Criteria**:

1. Decision is start controlled expansion, hold, rollback, or remediate.
2. Decision uses current approval, provider/mobile/support evidence, cohort setup, smoke results, revenue, learning, and incident readiness.
3. Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
4. v7.1 receives cohort scope, operating cadence, dashboards, owners, support coverage, and rollback controls only if expansion starts.
