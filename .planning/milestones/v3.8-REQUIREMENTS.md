Roll out full curriculum structure and exercise bank coverage for math, physics, German, and English on top of the existing subject/topic/practice foundations.

## Product Build Priority

This milestone makes the learning product feel complete across subjects by turning existing topic/profile/practice foundations into navigable curriculum content and exercise banks. It prioritizes usable curriculum coverage over billing, broad compliance, or production notification infrastructure.

## Out Of Scope

- Full adaptive sequencing engine.
- Rich curriculum authoring workflow with approvals and versioned publishing.
- Payment-provider implementation.
