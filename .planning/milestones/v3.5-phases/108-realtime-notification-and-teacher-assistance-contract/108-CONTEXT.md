# Phase 108 Context: Realtime Notification And Teacher Assistance Contract

**Milestone:** v3.5 Realtime And Teacher Assistance Foundation
**Requirement:** NOTIFY-01
**Status:** Planned

## Why This Phase Exists

`stoa_docs` Phase 2 calls for WebSocket realtime notifications and AI teacher assistance tools. The product needs a stable event and summary contract before adding broader realtime infrastructure or automatic exercise generation.

## Product Scope

- Notification event model for key student, tutor, parent, admin, subscription, moderation, and learning workflows.
- Read/archive lifecycle for in-product notifications.
- Teacher assistance summary seed contract for tutor workflows.
- Explicit boundary before full WebSocket rollout and autonomous exercise generation.

## Completion Criteria

Phase 108 completes when the notification event contract, teacher assistance seed contract, data model assumptions, UI implications, and lightweight verification checklist are written and internally consistent.
