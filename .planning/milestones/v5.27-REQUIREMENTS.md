# Requirements: v5.27 Pilot Remediation Product Fit And Reliability Hardening

**Milestone:** v5.27
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.26 Limited Pilot Execution And Outcome Evidence

## Purpose

Use real pilot evidence to fix the highest-impact product, reliability, support, AI, curriculum, and mobile issues before expanding the cohort.

## Requirements

### REMEDIATE-01 Pilot Issue Triage And Remediation Backlog

Acceptance criteria:

- v5.26 issues are grouped by severity, frequency, owner, affected role, product surface, and expansion impact.
- Backlog separates must-fix expansion blockers from acceptable known limitations and future enhancements.
- Each must-fix item has evidence source and verification plan.
- Unsupported or deferred issues are explained in pilot-facing/support-facing terms.

### REMEDIATE-02 Account Billing Mobile And Notification Remediation

Acceptance criteria:

- High-impact account, verification, entitlement, billing, mobile install/session/offline, and notification delivery issues are fixed or explicitly disabled.
- Fixes preserve no-demo-fallback behavior and support-safe state explanations.
- Regression tests cover reproduced issues.
- Support/admin views expose repaired state clearly.

### REMEDIATE-03 Learning AI Curriculum And Teacher-Help Remediation

Acceptance criteria:

- AI quality, exercise generation, assignment relevance, curriculum placement, teacher-help routing, and parent/student explanation issues are remediated or scoped.
- Teacher oversight and safety escalation remain intact.
- Learning fixes are validated against rubric fixtures, pilot examples, or approved synthetic fixtures.
- Raw student work is not copied into public docs or analytics evidence.

### REMEDIATE-04 Pilot Regression And Reliability Evidence

Acceptance criteria:

- Focused tests and smoke checks cover fixed pilot issues.
- Reliability evidence covers impacted APIs, frontend/mobile flows, provider states, and support operations.
- Dashboards or support exports show issue status, reopen rate, and remaining blockers.
- Release notes explain changes in parent/student/operator language.

### VERIFY-61 Remediation Release Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Must-fix expansion blockers are resolved or explicitly accepted by owner.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.28 expansion scope is based on resolved pilot evidence.
- If remediation remains insufficient, next milestone is another remediation cycle rather than expansion.

## Out of Scope

- Expanding cohort before must-fix blockers are resolved.
- Broad redesign unrelated to pilot evidence.
- Public launch.
- Unreviewed AI autonomy expansion.
- Provider or billing behavior that bypasses rollout controls.
