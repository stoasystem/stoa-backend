# Requirements: v3.4 Learning Expansion Foundation

**Milestone:** v3.4

## Goal

Prepare Phase 2 learning expansion without jumping directly into a broad curriculum rollout.

| Requirement | Phase | Status |

## Product Build Priority

This milestone creates reusable product foundations: subject taxonomy, topic metadata, prompt behavior by subject, and student learning profile seeds. It intentionally stops before full curriculum content, automatic exercise generation, and long-term personalization.

## Out Of Scope

- Full physics/German/English curriculum rollout.
- Automatic exercise generation.
- Payment-provider implementation.
- WebSocket realtime notifications.
- Full mobile/multilingual redesign.
