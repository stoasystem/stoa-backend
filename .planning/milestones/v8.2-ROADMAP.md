# Roadmap: v8.2 Revenue Growth Acquisition And Paid Marketing Gate

**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v8.1 Live Rollout Operations And Incident Remediation

## Goal

Evaluate and execute the next growth step only if rollout operations are healthy. v8.2 focuses on revenue conversion, retention, acquisition quality, referral/waitlist intake, lifecycle messaging, support capacity, pricing, and paid marketing approval gate.

## Function Purpose

- Prove that growth can happen without breaking support, billing, learning quality, or operations.
- Keep paid marketing spend separately gated by real capacity and economics.
- Improve conversion, retention, and acquisition quality from live evidence.

## Implementation Strategy

- Reconcile revenue, retention, churn, refunds, failed payments, usage/quota, pricing, lifecycle, and support corrections.
- Evaluate referral/waitlist/acquisition quality and support capacity.
- Gate paid marketing by support capacity, revenue quality, learning quality, incident readiness, and owner approval.
- Close with growth scale, organic-only growth, paid marketing prep, hold, rollback, or remediation.

## Phase Details

### Phase 457: Revenue Conversion Retention Evidence Review

**Goal**: Review conversion, retention, churn, refunds, payment failures, quota, pricing, lifecycle, and support corrections.
**Depends on**: v8.1 Live Rollout Operations And Incident Remediation.
**Requirements**: V8GROWTH-01
**Success Criteria**:

1. Conversion, retention, churn, refunds, failed payments, usage/quota, pricing, lifecycle, support corrections, and revenue reconciliation are reviewed.
2. Revenue evidence distinguishes real customers from internal/test accounts.
3. Parent copy and support copy match actual product behavior.
4. Revenue risks have owner, mitigation, and decision status.

### Phase 458: Acquisition Referral Waitlist Quality Review

**Goal**: Review referral, waitlist, invite, acquisition source quality, fraud/abuse signals, and cohort fit.
**Depends on**: Phase 457.
**Requirements**: V8GROWTH-02
**Success Criteria**:

1. Referral, waitlist, invite, acquisition source quality, cohort fit, and fraud/abuse signals are reviewed.
2. Intake is capped by support, teacher, billing, mobile, provider, incident, and learning quality capacity.
3. Acquisition reporting separates organic, referral, waitlist, partner, internal, and paid sources.
4. Poor-fit or high-risk intake paths are blocked or remediated.

### Phase 459: Growth Support Capacity And Lifecycle Fixes

**Goal**: Fix support capacity, lifecycle messaging, billing support, onboarding, and retention gaps.
**Depends on**: Phase 458.
**Requirements**: V8GROWTH-03
**Success Criteria**:

1. Support capacity, lifecycle messaging, billing support, onboarding, activation, reminder, renewal, failed payment, cancellation, and win-back gaps are fixed or deferred.
2. Preferences, locale, opt-out, failed delivery, refund, and escalation paths are handled.
3. Support staffing and teacher capacity are aligned with growth limits.
4. Fixes have tests, release evidence, and support-visible copy.

### Phase 460: Paid Marketing Approval And Experiment Design Gate

**Goal**: Decide whether paid marketing experiments are approved, scoped, budgeted, reversible, and support-ready.
**Depends on**: Phase 459.
**Requirements**: V8GROWTH-04
**Success Criteria**:

1. Paid marketing experiments, if proposed, have owner approval, budget, target audience, capacity gate, success metrics, stop criteria, and rollback/disable controls.
2. Paid spend remains blocked if support, revenue, learning quality, incident, mobile, or provider evidence is unhealthy.
3. Marketing copy does not hide known limitations or disabled features.
4. Experiment evidence is auditable.

### Phase 461: Growth Decision Gate

**Goal**: Decide growth scale, organic-only growth, paid marketing prep, hold, rollback, or remediation.
**Depends on**: Phase 460.
**Requirements**: VERIFY-91
**Success Criteria**:

1. Decision is growth scale, organic-only growth, paid marketing prep, hold, rollback, or remediation.
2. Decision uses revenue, retention, acquisition, support, teacher, learning, mobile, provider, incident, and release evidence.
3. Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
4. v8.3 receives learning-quality risks from growth evidence.
