# Requirements: v5.31 Real Limited Pilot Execution Operations

**Milestone:** v5.31
**Status:** Contract Complete
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.30 Live Pilot Approval And Provider Activation Execution

## Purpose

Run the first approved live limited pilot and produce real outcome evidence. v5.31 must stay controlled: no expansion, public launch, paid marketing, or uncontrolled provider writes.

## Requirements

### LIVEPILOTRUN-01 Live Cohort Enablement And Onboarding Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- Only approved pilot users are enabled.
- Onboarding tracks account verification, entitlement, child binding, curriculum placement, mobile/web access, notification preference, support path, and first learning action.
- Failed states have owner and support-visible next action.
- Cohort enablement can pause immediately.

### LIVEPILOTRUN-02 Daily Pilot Operations And Incident Review

Acceptance criteria:

- Daily checks cover auth, billing, usage/quota, AI, notifications, support, mobile, providers, and incidents.
- Incident and rollback thresholds are reviewed.
- Support issues are triaged with taxonomy and owner.
- Serious incidents pause cohort enablement.

### LIVEPILOTRUN-03 Live Learning Feedback And Support Quality Capture

Acceptance criteria:

- Parent/student/teacher/support feedback is captured through approved channels.
- AI quality, curriculum placement, teacher-help response, and parent explanations are reviewed.
- Evidence avoids raw private content in docs and exports.
- Findings are tagged for remediation or accepted limitation.

### LIVEPILOTRUN-04 Live Pilot Outcome Analysis

Acceptance criteria:

- Metrics cover activation, retention, usage, teacher-help response, AI quality, notification delivery, support load, mobile stability, account/billing friction, and satisfaction.
- Results are compared against v5.24/v5.26 criteria.
- Findings are grouped into expansion blockers, remediation backlog, accepted risks, and future improvements.
- Evidence is reproducible from dashboards, exports, or recorded support-safe snapshots.

### VERIFY-65 Live Pilot Decision Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is one of: continue pilot, pause, roll back, remediate before continuing, or expansion candidate.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.32 remediation scope is based on live pilot evidence.
- Expansion remains blocked until high-severity live pilot issues are resolved or accepted.

## Out of Scope

- Expanding beyond approved cohort.
- Public launch.
- Paid acquisition campaigns.
- Unapproved customer-impacting provider writes.
- Publishing raw feedback or student content.
