## Goal

Implement subscription checkout, webhook-driven billing state, parent payment UX, and admin billing visibility for STOA's first payment-provider integration.

## Product Build Priority

This milestone closes the largest remaining `stoa_docs` business-function gap: Phase 2 subscription payments after manual onboarding. It prioritizes working checkout, status, webhook, and billing UI over broad security/compliance evidence.

## Out Of Scope

- Real production charges without approved provider credentials.
- Tax/accounting automation.
- Refund operations.
- Full dunning/invoice lifecycle.
- Native mobile or notification provider rollout.
