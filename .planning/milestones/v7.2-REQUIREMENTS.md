# Requirements: v7.2 Public Launch Preparation And Acquisition Readiness

**Milestone:** v7.2
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v7.1 Controlled Expansion Operations And Remediation

## Purpose

Prepare public launch only when controlled expansion evidence supports it. v7.2 must produce a launch preparation package or a clear hold/remediation decision.

## Requirements

### V7LAUNCHPREP-01 Launch Scope Pricing Package And Copy Readiness

Acceptance criteria:

- Audience, rollout scope, pricing/package, plan limits, disabled features, known limitations, eligibility, and support policy are final.
- Parent/student/teacher/admin copy is ready for included and excluded features.
- Pricing and billing support copy match actual entitlement and usage behavior.
- Paid marketing approval is tracked separately from launch prep.

### V7LAUNCHPREP-02 Web Mobile App Store And Provider Launch Readiness

Acceptance criteria:

- Frontend/web, mobile/app-store, backend, payment, notification, support, BI/APM, AI/provider, and monitoring readiness are verified.
- App-store constraints and mobile limitations are explicit.
- Provider failures have disablement and fallback controls.
- Release evidence links code SHA, deploy/build IDs, request IDs where applicable, timestamp, and owner.

### V7LAUNCHPREP-03 Support Lifecycle Acquisition And Capacity Readiness

Acceptance criteria:

- Support staffing, incident coverage, lifecycle messages, referral/waitlist intake, acquisition limits, retention reporting, and escalation paths are ready.
- Growth intake is capped by support, teacher, billing, mobile, provider, and incident capacity.
- Support macros cover billing, login, learning, mobile, AI/provider, notification, and refund issues.
- Preferences, locale, delivery failure, and opt-out behavior are handled.

### V7LAUNCHPREP-04 Launch Freeze Rollback Dashboard And Evidence Package

Acceptance criteria:

- Launch freeze, staged rollout, rollback, dashboards, alerts, incident runbooks, owner approvals, and evidence package are current.
- Dashboards cover auth, entitlement, usage, billing, notification, support, teacher, AI/provider, mobile, learning, incidents, revenue, and retention.
- Known residual risks have owner, mitigation, and go/no-go status.
- Evidence excludes secrets, raw prompts, raw student content, private object keys, and raw provider payloads.

### VERIFY-86 v7.2 Launch Preparation Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is launch-ready, controlled-expansion-only, hold, rollback, or remediation.
- Decision uses expansion, revenue, learning, support, mobile, provider, incident, acquisition, and release evidence.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v7.3 proceeds only with launch-ready evidence and final owner approval.

## Out of Scope

- Public launch execution.
- Paid marketing execution.
- Expanding launch scope beyond approved capacity.
- Hiding disabled features or known limitations.
