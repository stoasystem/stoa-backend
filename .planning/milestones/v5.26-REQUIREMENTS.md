# Requirements: v5.26 Limited Pilot Execution And Outcome Evidence

**Milestone:** v5.26
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.25 Pilot Activation Blocker Burn-Down And Safe Start Decision

## Purpose

Execute the limited pilot only after v5.25 safe-start approval, then collect the operational and product evidence needed to decide whether STOA should continue, pause, roll back, remediate, or expand.

## Requirements

### PILOTRUN-01 Pilot Cohort Enablement And First-Use Tracking

Acceptance criteria:

- Approved cohort is enabled through documented rollout flags and cohort records.
- Parent/student onboarding, email verification, entitlement, child binding, curriculum placement, mobile/web access, and support path are tracked.
- First successful learning action is measured without exposing raw student content.
- Failed onboarding states have support-visible next actions.

### PILOTRUN-02 Daily Pilot Monitoring And Incident Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- Daily launch-room checklist covers auth, billing/entitlement, usage/quota, AI, notifications, support, mobile, providers, and incidents.
- Alert, incident, rollback, and disabled-feature states are reviewed and recorded.
- Support issues are triaged with issue taxonomy and owner.
- Serious incidents can pause cohort enablement or trigger rollback.

### PILOTRUN-03 Pilot Support Feedback And Learning Quality Evidence

Acceptance criteria:

- Parent, student, teacher/tutor, admin, and support feedback is collected through approved channels.
- AI summary/exercise/assignment quality and teacher-help response quality are reviewed against v5.21 rubrics.
- Curriculum placement, progress explanation, and practice relevance issues are recorded.
- Feedback evidence remains support-safe and avoids raw private content.

### PILOTRUN-04 Pilot Metrics Outcome Analysis

Acceptance criteria:

- Metrics cover activation, retention, usage, teacher-help response, AI quality, notification delivery, support load, mobile stability, account/billing friction, and satisfaction.
- Results are compared to v5.24 success/hold/rollback criteria.
- Findings are grouped into expansion blockers, remediation backlog, accepted risks, and future improvements.
- Evidence is reproducible from dashboards, exports, or recorded support-safe snapshots.

### VERIFY-60 Pilot Outcome Decision Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is one of: continue pilot, pause, roll back, remediate before continuing, or expand candidate.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.27 remediation scope is based on real pilot evidence, not speculative backlog.
- Any expansion is blocked until high-severity pilot issues are resolved or explicitly accepted.

## Out of Scope

- Expanding beyond the approved cohort.
- Public launch.
- Paid acquisition campaigns.
- Uncontrolled provider writes or customer-impacting changes outside rollout controls.
- Raw content analytics or private feedback publication.
