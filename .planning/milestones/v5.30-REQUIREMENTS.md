# Requirements: v5.30 Live Pilot Approval And Provider Activation Execution

**Milestone:** v5.30
**Status:** Active
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.25-v5.29 Pilot Launch Sequence Contract Completion

## Purpose

Turn locally complete pilot contracts into a live, approved pilot start or a precise hold decision. v5.30 must obtain explicit operational approval and live/read-only evidence for every required dependency before any real pilot cohort is enabled.

## Requirements

### LIVEPILOT-01 Live Approval And Ownership Audit

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision owner, product owner, support owner, incident owner, finance/billing owner, mobile release owner, and provider owners are recorded.
- Pilot cohort scope, disabled features, accepted risks, support hours, and rollback authority are approved.
- Required credentials and environments are classified as approved, missing, disabled, or blocked.
- No real-user pilot action proceeds without recorded approval.

### LIVEPILOT-02 Live Provider And Mobile Activation Evidence

Acceptance criteria:

- Payment, notifications, support CRM, BI/APM, and mobile/TestFlight readiness have redacted live/read-only evidence or explicit pilot disablement.
- Evidence includes timestamp, owner, request ID/build ID where applicable, result, blocker state, and rollback/disable control.
- Disabled dependencies include user/support copy and impact.
- Evidence excludes secrets, tokens, raw provider payloads, raw student content, private object keys, and private feedback.

### LIVEPILOT-03 Production Restore Tabletop And Launch-Room Evidence

Acceptance criteria:

- Production restore/readback or approved tabletop evidence is recorded for pilot-critical data.
- Launch-room rehearsal covers auth, billing, notification, support, AI/provider issue, mobile incident, and rollback.
- Incident severity, escalation path, pause criteria, and rollback criteria are approved.
- Remaining gaps are classified as start-blocking, accepted risk, or disabled from pilot.

### LIVEPILOT-04 Live Pilot Safe-Start Gate Execution

Acceptance criteria:

- `pilot_safe_start_gate` or equivalent evidence is executed with the current live evidence state.
- Decision is one of: `start_limited_pilot`, `hold`, or `harden_further`.
- Decision packet includes blocker table, live evidence, disablement list, accepted risks, and owner signoff.
- v5.31 cannot execute real pilot activity unless decision is `start_limited_pilot`.

### VERIFY-64 Live Activation Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.31 scope is adjusted to real pilot execution or continued hold/remediation based on the live gate.
- Public launch, paid marketing, and uncontrolled provider writes remain out of scope.
- Worktree evidence remains metadata-only and support-safe.

## Out of Scope

- Real-user pilot execution before `start_limited_pilot`.
- Controlled expansion.
- Public launch.
- Paid marketing.
- Unapproved provider writes or customer-impacting changes outside approved pilot controls.
