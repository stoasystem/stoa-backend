# Roadmap: v5.37 Revenue Conversion And Self-Serve Growth Completion

**Status:** Contract Complete
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.36 Live Pilot Operations Feedback And Product Fixes

## Goal

Complete the paid conversion and self-serve growth loop using real pilot evidence: packaging, checkout, entitlement activation, billing support, lifecycle messaging, referral/waitlist, and revenue reconciliation.

## Function Purpose

- Turn payment and entitlement from readiness surfaces into a dependable parent-facing purchase and renewal flow.
- Make account value, quota usage, subscription state, and billing support visible enough for real customers.
- Build measured growth loops only after support and billing capacity can absorb them.

## Implementation Strategy

- Use pilot conversion friction and support tickets as the prioritization input.
- Keep all revenue changes reconciled against entitlement and usage ledger state.
- Make lifecycle messaging opt-in/preference-aware and support-visible.
- Close with controlled growth readiness, not broad paid marketing by default.
