# Roadmap: v8.3 Learning Outcomes Scale And AI Curriculum Improvement

**Status:** Completed
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v8.2 Revenue Growth Acquisition And Paid Marketing Gate

## Goal

Protect and improve learning outcomes as rollout and growth increase. v8.3 focuses on real learning progress, weak-topic remediation, curriculum quality, AI teacher quality, adaptive recommendations, parent progress, teacher workload, and support burden.

## Function Purpose

- Make growth depend on actual learning value.
- Prevent acquisition from masking weak curriculum, AI, support, or teacher workflows.
- Keep curriculum authorization and AI review boundaries intact.

## Implementation Strategy

- Review learning outcomes across cohorts and acquisition sources.
- Rank issues by student impact, frequency, parent confusion, teacher workload, support burden, and retention impact.
- Ship curriculum, AI, recommendation, parent progress, and teacher workflow improvements with tests and evidence.
- Close with learning scale, hold, remediation, content/AI freeze, or strategic pivot.

## Phase Details

### Phase 462: Scaled Learning Outcome And Cohort Analysis

**Goal**: Analyze progress, mastery, weak topics, completion, retry, support contacts, teacher help, retention, and cohort/source differences.
**Depends on**: v8.2 Revenue Growth Acquisition And Paid Marketing Gate.
**Requirements**: V8LEARN-01
**Success Criteria**:

1. Evidence covers progress, mastery, weak topics, completion, retry, support contacts, teacher help, parent report engagement, retention, and acquisition/cohort source differences.
2. Learning issues are separated from billing, login, support, notification, and onboarding issues.
3. Top issues are ranked by student impact, frequency, parent confusion, teacher workload, support burden, and retention impact.
4. Evidence excludes raw private student content and raw prompts.

### Phase 463: Curriculum Exercise Recommendation Quality Release

**Goal**: Improve curriculum, exercises, explanations, sequencing, recommendations, and parent progress reporting.
**Depends on**: Phase 462.
**Requirements**: V8LEARN-02
**Success Criteria**:

1. Curriculum, exercises, explanations, sequencing, recommendations, and parent progress reporting are improved for highest-impact gaps.
2. Curriculum editing remains specially authorized.
3. Changed content has validation, preview, rollback metadata, analytics tags, and support/teacher notes.
4. Recommendations preserve freshness, dedupe, and family-understandable rationale.

### Phase 464: AI Teacher Quality Safety And Cost Release

**Goal**: Improve AI summaries, practice generation, explanations, refusal/fallback, safety, cost, latency, and teacher review.
**Depends on**: Phase 463.
**Requirements**: V8LEARN-03
**Success Criteria**:

1. AI summaries, practice generation, explanations, teacher tools, refusal/fallback, safety, cost, latency, and provider errors are improved.
2. Low-quality, unsafe, off-topic, or overconfident outputs are caught by review, refusal, or fallback behavior.
3. Teacher review supports accept, edit, reject, explain, and follow-up workflows efficiently.
4. AI autonomy remains reviewed or policy-bound.

### Phase 465: Teacher Workload Parent Clarity And Support Reduction

**Goal**: Reduce teacher load, parent confusion, support burden, and repeated learning friction.
**Depends on**: Phase 464.
**Requirements**: V8LEARN-04
**Success Criteria**:

1. Teacher workload, parent confusion, repeated support contacts, unclear next steps, and repeated learning friction are reduced.
2. Parent/student/teacher copy is updated where product behavior changes.
3. Support macros reflect new learning behavior.
4. Improvements have tests and outcome evidence.

### Phase 466: Learning Scale Decision Gate

**Goal**: Decide learning scale, hold, remediation, content/AI freeze, or strategic pivot.
**Depends on**: Phase 465.
**Requirements**: VERIFY-92
**Success Criteria**:

1. Decision is learning scale, hold, remediation, content/AI freeze, or strategic pivot.
2. Decision uses learning, retention, support, teacher, parent, AI/provider, curriculum, and cohort evidence.
3. Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
4. v8.4 receives strategic risks and opportunities from learning and growth evidence.
