# Roadmap: v8.3 Learning Outcomes Scale And AI Curriculum Improvement

**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-09
**Prior milestone:** v8.2 Revenue Growth Acquisition And Paid Marketing Gate

## Goal

Protect and improve learning outcomes as rollout and growth increase. v8.3 focuses on real learning progress, weak-topic remediation, curriculum quality, AI teacher quality, adaptive recommendations, parent progress, teacher workload, and support burden.

## Function Purpose

- Make growth depend on actual learning value.
- Prevent acquisition from masking weak curriculum, AI, support, or teacher workflows.
- Keep curriculum authorization and AI review boundaries intact.

## Implementation Strategy

- Review learning outcomes across cohorts and acquisition sources.
- Rank issues by student impact, frequency, parent confusion, teacher workload, support burden, and retention impact.
- Ship curriculum, AI, recommendation, parent progress, and teacher workflow improvements with tests and evidence.
- Close with learning scale, hold, remediation, content/AI freeze, or strategic pivot.

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 462: Scaled Learning Outcome And Cohort Analysis** - Analyze progress, mastery, weak topics, completion, retry, support contacts, teacher help, retention, and cohort/source differences.
- [ ] **Phase 463: Curriculum Exercise Recommendation Quality Release** - Improve curriculum, exercises, explanations, sequencing, recommendations, and parent progress reporting.
- [ ] **Phase 464: AI Teacher Quality Safety And Cost Release** - Improve AI summaries, practice generation, explanations, refusal/fallback, safety, cost, latency, and teacher review.
- [ ] **Phase 465: Teacher Workload Parent Clarity And Support Reduction** - Reduce teacher load, parent confusion, support burden, and repeated learning friction.
- [ ] **Phase 466: v8.3 Learning Scale Decision Gate** - Decide learning scale, hold, remediation, content/AI freeze, or strategic pivot.
