# Requirements: v5.28 Controlled Expansion Revenue And Operations Scale

**Milestone:** v5.28
**Status:** Contract Complete
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.27 Pilot Remediation Product Fit And Reliability Hardening

## Purpose

Move from a narrow pilot to controlled expansion only after remediation evidence supports it. v5.28 validates revenue operations, support/teacher capacity, mobile/provider readiness, and operational scale under a larger but still gated cohort.

## Requirements

### EXPAND-01 Expansion Cohort And Capacity Plan

Acceptance criteria:

- Expanded cohort size, user mix, rollout stages, rollback thresholds, and excluded features are defined.
- Capacity assumptions cover API, mobile, providers, AI, teacher-help, support, billing, BI/APM, and incident response.
- Cohort communication and consent updates are prepared.
- Expansion does not start while unresolved v5.27 blockers remain.

### EXPAND-02 Revenue Billing And Subscription Operations Scale

Acceptance criteria:

- Payment, entitlement, refunds, failed payments, dunning, accounting handoff, and billing support flows are verified for expanded use.
- Billing support views expose provider state without raw provider payloads or secrets.
- Finance/support ownership and reconciliation cadence are documented.
- Revenue metrics are included in expansion dashboard.

### EXPAND-03 Teacher Support And Customer Operations Scale

Acceptance criteria:

- Teacher-help queue, SLA, dispatch, escalation, support CRM, lifecycle messaging, and feedback workflows are scaled to expansion assumptions.
- Staffing plan covers normal hours, spike handling, and escalation ownership.
- Parent/student support copy is updated for expansion scope.
- Support load and teacher response quality are measured.

### EXPAND-04 Mobile Provider And Infrastructure Scale Smoke

Acceptance criteria:

- Mobile release channel, push delivery, offline/cache behavior, auth/session, and crash/performance signals are checked under expanded use.
- Provider capacity and BI/APM dashboards are reviewed.
- Infrastructure runbooks cover scaling, rollback, and provider degradation.
- Evidence remains redacted and support-safe.

### VERIFY-62 Controlled Expansion Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is one of: continue expansion, hold, remediate, or prepare public-launch readiness.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- v5.29 scope is adjusted based on expansion outcomes.
- Public launch remains blocked unless expansion metrics and support capacity meet thresholds.

## Out of Scope

- Unbounded public signup.
- Paid marketing campaign execution.
- Enterprise sales automation.
- Ignoring support/teacher capacity thresholds.
- Bypassing billing/provider rollout controls.
