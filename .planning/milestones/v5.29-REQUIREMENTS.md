# Requirements: v5.29 Public Launch Readiness Growth And Self-Serve Onboarding

**Milestone:** v5.29
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.28 Controlled Expansion Revenue And Operations Scale

## Purpose

Prepare STOA for public launch or a larger market-facing release only if controlled expansion evidence supports it. v5.29 completes self-serve onboarding, pricing/package readiness, growth/lifecycle loops, public support operations, app-store readiness, and final launch controls.

## Requirements

### LAUNCH-01 Self-Serve Onboarding And Account Conversion

Acceptance criteria:

- Self-serve signup, email verification, parent/student setup, entitlement, payment/subscription, mobile install, and first-learning-action flow is defined and implemented or clearly scoped.
- Conversion events and support fallback are visible.
- Account recovery and blocked states have user-safe explanations.
- No demo fallback or hidden manual entitlement path is used for public users.

### LAUNCH-02 Pricing Packaging Growth And Lifecycle Readiness

Acceptance criteria:

- Pricing/package display, plan comparison, subscription state, upgrade/downgrade/cancel, and billing support copy are launch-ready.
- Growth loops such as waitlist, referral, lifecycle nudges, or trial conversion are scoped with opt-out/preference controls.
- Retention reporting ties onboarding, learning activity, notifications, support, and billing state together.
- Growth work does not bypass privacy, consent, or support-capacity gates.

### LAUNCH-03 Public Support Knowledge Base And Launch Communications

Acceptance criteria:

- Public support docs cover onboarding, billing, verification, mobile install, notifications, AI limits, teacher help, privacy, and incident status.
- Launch communications include release notes, known limitations, escalation paths, and rollback/hold communication.
- Support team has macros/templates and escalation ownership.
- Content is accurate for enabled features and explicitly disabled features.

### LAUNCH-04 App Store Public Release And Production Launch Controls

Acceptance criteria:

- App-store/public release assets, review notes, privacy declarations, notification usage, screenshots, and version metadata are prepared.
- Production launch dashboard covers auth, billing, usage/quota, AI, notifications, support, mobile, provider blockers, incidents, and revenue.
- Launch freeze, staged rollout, rollback, provider disablement, and support staffing are approved.
- Provider capacity and data lifecycle checks are current.

### VERIFY-63 Public Launch Readiness Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Decision is one of: public launch, continue controlled expansion, hold, or harden further.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- Accepted risks and launch blockers are explicit.
- If public launch is approved, v5.30 should be launch execution and post-launch operations; otherwise v5.30 should address the blocking category.

## Out of Scope

- Public launch without final gate approval.
- Paid marketing spend without support and provider capacity approval.
- Enterprise sales automation.
- Unreviewed AI autonomy expansion.
- Hiding disabled features or known blockers from users.
