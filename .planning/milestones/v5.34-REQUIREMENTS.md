# Requirements: v5.34 Public Launch Execution And Post-Launch Operations

**Milestone:** v5.34
**Status:** Planned
**Created:** 2026-07-07
**Prior milestone:** v5.33 Controlled Expansion Execution And Revenue Validation

## Purpose

Execute public launch only if final approval exists; otherwise continue controlled expansion or hold. v5.34 also establishes post-launch operations for support, incidents, revenue, mobile, AI/curriculum quality, and growth feedback.

## Requirements

### LAUNCHEXEC-01 Final Launch Approval And Public Rollout Plan

Acceptance criteria:

- Final decision is public launch, continued controlled expansion, hold, or rollback.
- Scope, rollout stages, support staffing, provider capacity, billing readiness, app-store state, and rollback authority are approved.
- Known limitations and disabled features are documented.
- Public launch does not proceed without owner signoff and final launch gate approval.

### LAUNCHEXEC-02 Self-Serve Onboarding Growth And Support Launch

Acceptance criteria:

- Self-serve signup, verification, parent/student setup, subscription, mobile install, and first-learning-action flow are enabled for launch scope.
- Pricing/package, referral/waitlist/lifecycle, and retention reporting are live or explicitly scoped out.
- Support docs, macros, escalation paths, and incident communications are current.
- Growth loops respect consent, preferences, privacy, and support capacity.

### LAUNCHEXEC-03 App Store Production Release And Launch Monitoring

Acceptance criteria:

- App-store/public release assets, review notes, privacy declarations, screenshots, and version metadata are approved.
- Production launch dashboard covers auth, billing, usage/quota, AI, notifications, support, mobile, provider blockers, incidents, and revenue.
- Launch freeze, staged rollout, rollback, provider disablement, and support staffing are active.
- Monitoring evidence excludes raw student content, prompts, answers, tokens, secrets, private keys, and raw provider payloads.

### LAUNCHEXEC-04 Post-Launch Incident Revenue And Learning Operations

Acceptance criteria:

- Post-launch cadence covers incidents, revenue reconciliation, support load, teacher response, AI quality, curriculum issues, mobile crashes, provider health, and retention.
- Issues are triaged into hotfix, support action, product backlog, growth learning, or accepted limitation.
- Revenue and support reports reconcile with provider/account-operation state.
- Launch learnings feed v5.35 recommendation.

### VERIFY-68 Launch Outcome And Next Strategy Gate

Acceptance criteria:

- Launch or controlled-expansion outcome report is recorded.
- Roadmap, requirements, state, milestone snapshots, and project summary are updated.
- Decision is scale, hold, rollback, or next focused product/growth milestone.
- v5.35 recommendation is based on live evidence.

## Out of Scope

- Public launch without final approval.
- Paid marketing spend beyond approved launch scope.
- Enterprise sales automation.
- Unreviewed AI autonomy expansion.
- Hiding disabled features or known blockers from users.
